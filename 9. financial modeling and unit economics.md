**ROLE & CONTEXT**

Act as a VP of Finance with 15+ years of experience at high-growth startups and prior consulting experience at a Tier-1 strategy firm. You think in structured frameworks, communicate with precision, and never present a number without the assumption that drives it.

> My business: `[BUSINESS MODEL — e.g., B2B SaaS, D2C subscription, marketplace]`
> Current MRR/ARR: `[REVENUE]`

> Growth rate (MoM or YoY): `[GROWTH RATE]`

**DELIVERABLE 1 — UNIT ECONOMICS DASHBOARD**

Build a complete unit economics model with explicit formulas and stated assumptions for every metric:

① **CAC by channel** (paid, organic, referral, outbound) — show channel-level and blended CAC.
_Formula: Total Sales & Marketing Spend ÷ New Customers Acquired_

② **LTV with full assumption table** — ARPU, gross margin %, churn rate, average customer lifespan.
_Formula: (ARPU × Gross Margin %) ÷ Monthly Churn Rate_

③ **LTV:CAC ratio and payback period** in months. Flag if ratio falls below 3:1 or payback exceeds 18 months.

④ **Gross margin per unit/customer** — distinguish revenue margin from contribution margin before and after all variable costs.

⑤ **Contribution margin analysis** — Revenue minus COGS, customer success, hosting, and payment fees. Express as both $ and %.

---

**DELIVERABLE 2 — 3-YEAR FINANCIAL PROJECTION**

- **Year 1:** Monthly P&L (Jan–Dec) — Revenue, COGS, Gross Profit, Opex (S&M / R&D / G&A), EBITDA, Net Cash Flow
- **Years 2–3:** Quarterly rollup with the same line items
- **Cost structure:** Separate fixed costs (salaries, rent, software) from variable costs (CAC, COGS, payment processing). Show margin improvement with scale.
- **Break-even analysis:** The exact month and customer/revenue volume where the business hits contribution margin break-even and EBITDA break-even
- **Cash flow forecast:** Monthly opening/closing cash balance, burn rate, and runway in months assuming `[CURRENT CASH]` in the bank

---

**DELIVERABLE 3 — SCENARIO & SENSITIVITY ANALYSIS**

Model three scenarios across two primary levers — CAC and churn rate:

| Scenario  | CAC vs. Base | Monthly Churn vs. Base | Purpose          |

| Bear Case | +30%         | +1.5 pp                | Survival floor   |
| Base Case | Current      | Current                | Expected outcome |
| Bull Case | −20%         | −0.5 pp                | Upside ceiling   |

Show Year 1 and Year 3 impact on Revenue, EBITDA, and cash runway for each scenario.

Compare all key metrics against industry benchmarks for `[INDUSTRY / BUSINESS MODEL]`.
