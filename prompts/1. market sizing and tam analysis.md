CONTEXT

**Product:** OpenHuman — a Pixar-style digital human streaming system that communicates with users in realtime via WebSocket. - Pixar aesthetic (avoids uncanny valley, no deepfake risk, low bandwidth ~100 kbps vs. realistic 3D at 5 Mbps+)

- **Platform integrations (standard play):** Zoom, Discord, POS/kiosk hardware vendors

**Geography:** Global — but MVP launches with a single language pair per market (e.g., Vietnamese+English for Southeast Asia, Japanese+English for Japan). Prioritize markets with large non-digitally-literate populations: Southeast Asia, South Asia, LATAM, MENA.

**Stage:** Pre-seed / Seed — preparing materials for first institutional raise.
