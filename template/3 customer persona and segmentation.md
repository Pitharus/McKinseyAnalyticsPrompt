**DELIVERABLE: 4 HIGH-RESOLUTION CUSTOMER PERSONAS**

For each persona, structure your analysis across 9 dimensions:

**1. Profile Overview**
— Name, archetype title, and one-sentence strategic summary
— Demographics: age range, income, education, job title/function, location tier

**2. Psychographic & Behavioral DNA**
— Core values, belief system, risk appetite, decision-making style
— Lifestyle patterns and identity anchors that drive category behavior

**3. Pain Architecture** _(Top 5)_
— Rank frustrations by frequency AND emotional intensity
— Distinguish _stated_ pains from _latent/unarticulated_ needs

**4. Goals & Success Metrics**
— Professional and personal aspirations
— How they define and measure "winning" in the next 12 months

**5. Purchase Journey Map**
— Discovery → Evaluation criteria → Decision triggers → Post-purchase behavior
— Key influencers and gatekeepers in the buying process

**6. Media & Channel Consumption**
— Online: platforms, content formats, peak usage windows
— Offline: events, publications, physical touchpoints

**7. Objection Register** _(Top 3)_
— Articulate each objection exactly as the customer would voice it
— Provide the optimal counter-narrative for each

**8. Trigger Events**
— Specific life or business events that activate purchase intent
— Urgency signals and behavioral indicators to monitor

**9. Willingness-to-Pay Analysis**
— Acceptable price range and preferred model _(subscription / one-time / usage-based)_
— Price sensitivity drivers and value anchors

---

**STRATEGIC SYNTHESIS** _(after all 4 personas)_

① **Segment sizing** — estimated % of total addressable market per persona
② **Revenue prioritization matrix** — rank by: LTV × acquisition cost × strategic fit
③ **GTM sequencing recommendation** — which segment to lead with, and why
④ **Cross-segment opportunity map** — shared messaging or product features that serve multiple personas

**FORMAT DIRECTIVE:** Use structured headers. Be precise and evidence-based. Avoid generic archetypes — ground every persona in behavioral patterns specific to `[INDUSTRY]`.
