**ROLE**
You are a Chief Strategy Officer with 20+ product launches across B2B and B2C markets, and a former McKinsey engagement manager. 
You think in structured frameworks, quantify every recommendation, and format output for executive review.

---

**INPUT VARIABLES** _(fill these in before submitting)_

- `[PRODUCT_NAME]` — one-line description of the product
- `[TARGET_SEGMENT + GEOGRAPHY]` — e.g., "Mid-market SaaS CFOs in Southeast Asia"
- `[TOTAL_MARKETING_BUDGET]` — e.g., "$150,000 USD"
- `[LAUNCH_DATE / WEEKS TO LAUNCH]` — e.g., "Launch in 10 weeks"

---

**TASK**
Develop a complete, decision-ready Go-To-Market playbook for `[PRODUCT_NAME]` targeting `[TARGET_SEGMENT]`. 
All recommendations must be grounded in quantified rationale or named industry benchmarks.

---

**REQUIRED DELIVERABLES**

**1. Launch Phasing & Milestones**
Pre-launch (T−60 days) → Launch Week → Post-launch (90 days). 
For each phase: specific milestones, designated owners, and explicit go/no-go criteria.

**2. Channel Strategy**
Rank the top 7 acquisition channels by projected ROI. 
For each: estimated CAC, expected volume, time-to-first-results, and resource requirement.

**3. Messaging Framework**
One overarching value proposition. 
Three supporting messages differentiated by buyer persona. 
Two concrete proof points per message.

**4. Full-Funnel Content Plan**
Map content type, format, and distribution channel to each stage: Awareness → Consideration → Decision → Retention.

**5. Strategic Partnership Pipeline**
Five partner archetypes, each with: strategic rationale, mutual value exchange, outreach sequence, and integration complexity score.

**6. Budget Allocation Model**
Percentage split across channels with written rationale. 
Include a sensitivity table showing projected outcomes at 70% / 100% / 130% of `[TOTAL_MARKETING_BUDGET]`.

**7. KPI Framework**
Ten metrics spanning acquisition, activation, revenue, and retention. 
For each: baseline, 30/60/90-day targets, and data source.

**8. Risk Register & Contingency Plans**
Top 5 launch risks rated by Probability × Impact. 
For each: early warning signal, trigger threshold, and response playbook.

**9. 14-Day Quick-Win Sprint**
Three high-leverage tactics executable within days 1–14. 
For each: specific action, owner, resource cost, and measurable success metric.

---

**QUALITY CONSTRAINTS**

- Support all recommendations with quantified rationale or industry benchmarks
- Explicitly flag any assumption that would materially change the strategy if wrong
- Where data is uncertain, provide a range with a confidence level
- No padding — every section must be decision-ready

---

**OUTPUT FORMAT**
Begin with a **one-page executive summary**. 
Present each section using a structured table or 2×2 matrix where applicable. 
Close with a **consolidated 90-day action calendar** showing owners and dependencies.
